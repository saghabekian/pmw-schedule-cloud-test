PMW Render v50.2 Unassigned Ticket Alert

Built from v50.1.

Adds:
- Red alert at top of Schedule page when tickets are not assigned.
- Shows count of tickets not added to Fabrication or Numbering.
- Click Open Tickets to view unassigned tickets.
- Green alert when all tickets are assigned.

A ticket is assigned when scheduled_status='scheduled', which happens after Add to Fabrication or Add to Numbering.
No other app features changed.
