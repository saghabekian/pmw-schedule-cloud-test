PMW Render v42 Sort Preserve Numbers

Corrects the sort rule.

Rule:
- Whatever number you type in the left/order box stays attached to that job.
- Sort uses that number to reorder the full 3-cell row.
- The 3 cells move together:
  sort number + job/description/email link + done/notes
- It does NOT auto-renumber after sorting.

Example:
Before:
7 | JAKES
6 | Supply
5 | Goose Neck

After Sort:
5 | Goose Neck
6 | Supply
7 | JAKES

This version is made to replace the bad v41 renumber behavior.
