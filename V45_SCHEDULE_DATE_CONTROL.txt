PMW Render v45 Schedule Date Control

Built from stable v44.

Adds:
- Schedule date at the top uses today's date automatically by default.
- Admin/editor can click Change Date.
- Choose Auto Today or Manual Date.
- Manual date is saved in PostgreSQL per schedule sheet.
- Auto Today updates to the current date automatically.

Test:
1. Deploy v45.
2. Confirm top date shows today's date.
3. Click Change Date.
4. Choose Manual Date and save.
5. Refresh and confirm it stays.
6. Switch back to Auto Today and confirm it shows current date.
