PMW Render v46.2 Auto Refresh Tabs

Adds:
- Refresh when users return to an old PMW tab after 45+ seconds away.
- Refresh every 5 minutes if no one is actively typing/editing.
- No refresh while user is focused in a cell/input or recently typed.
- Small notice after automatic refresh.

Purpose:
- Reduce stale browser tabs autosaving old schedule data over newer changes.
