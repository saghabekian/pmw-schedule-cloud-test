PMW Render v48 Job History

Built from v47 Ticket Cleanup.

Only adds:
- Admin-only Job History page
- Editor Mark Complete button

How it works:
- Editor clicks any cell in a schedule job row.
- Clicks Mark Complete.
- App extracts job number like 25-010 from the job description.
- Saves completion history:
  job number, stage, description, sheet, row, completed by, completed at.
- Marks the row's Done column with X in green.

Admin:
- Admin > Job History
- Search by job number, description, or user.
- Filter by Fabrication / Numbering.
