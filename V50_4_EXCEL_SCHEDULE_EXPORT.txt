PMW Render v50.4 Excel Schedule Export

Adds:
- Admin-only Export Schedule to Excel button.
- Route: /admin/export_schedule_excel
- Exports current schedule sheets to .xlsx.
- Includes schedule rows, sort numbers, job cells, done cells, colors, text colors, bold, font sizes, and attachment label text.

Notes:
- Internal app attachment links are exported as text labels because they depend on the live web app/database.
- This is a human-readable Excel backup that can be saved separately from the PostgreSQL JSON backup.
